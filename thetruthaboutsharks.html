Shark Attack Facts

Out of the 200 million Americans swim at the beach every year yet only about 36 are even attacked by sharks with less than 4 fatalities. Considering the large amount of beach-goers, instances of shark attacks are very rare. Since 2006 only about 4 fatal shark attacks have occured per year worldwide. In the US only 3 of the 179 shark attacks were fatal.
